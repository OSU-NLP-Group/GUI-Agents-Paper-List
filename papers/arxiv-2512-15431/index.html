TLDR

Step-GUI centers on a self-evolving training pipeline built around the Calibrated Step Reward System, which calibrates model-generated GUI trajectories against trajectory-level signals to produce high-quality supervision at much lower annotation cost. On top of that pipeline, the paper introduces 4B and 8B GUI specialist models, the GUI-MCP protocol, and the AndroidDaily benchmark.
